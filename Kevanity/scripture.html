High Gospel of Kevanity


Note that this scripture is just a humorous set of paragraphs our friends put together for the sake of having the scripture. Nothing here is meant to be sacriligeous or offensive to any relgious following or association. Please just try to find the humor in it all and don't find offense.

Book of England

The world is full of sadness and despair. However, the problem is not that people have nothing to look forward to. The problem is that people are only looking forward in time, and not appreciating what they have at the moment. People are so focused on what's going to happen in the long run and what's going to happen when their time here on Earth is up that they are not living a happy and full life. Kevanity has shown me the errors of this mindset. Kevanity has shown me that you need to love everything and be happy with what is going on NOW. Because we live in a world of uncertainty, a world that can be destroyed much quicker than it was created. A world where you do not know what comes next. All you know for fact is the things that are happening around you. You do not need to worry about what life you have after this one because you already know the difference between right and wrong and if you live this life to the best of your abilities then you are already starting whatever life you have after this one on the right foot. We may all be Followers of Kevanity but you will always have the choice on what you want to do. Kevin cannot make these choices for you. And if you have the choice to be happy, why would you not always be happy? I leave you all with this. Look to Kevin not to solve your problems. Look not to Kevin to guide you into some sort of better life after this one. Look to Kevin only to see that it is not as difficult as you might think to appreciate what you have here and now. The choice is yours, Kevinites. Shall you join Kevin in living in the present? Or will you spend this only guaranteed life waiting for the next one that is only hoped for?
